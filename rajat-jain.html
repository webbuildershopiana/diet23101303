    Profile

    As a Nutritionist and Dietitian with over 8 years of experience, I’m humbled to have been recognized as the “Best Dietitian in Rajasthan” six times and as one of the “Top 10 Most Promising Dietitians in India.” I’m also grateful to have had the opportunity to positively impact the health of over 4000+ individuals from various backgrounds, both locally and worldwide.

    In 2016, I founded the ReviveQii Diet Clinic (previously known as Health Wealth Diet Clinic), through which I offer online diet consultation, providing personalized diet plans and healthy meal plans for weight loss. My goal is to empower my clients to take charge of their health by equipping them with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed dietary choices.

    My expertise lies in weight loss, clinical and sports nutrition, diabetes education, and wellness education. I have had the privilege of working with prestigious organizations such as the Rajasthan Police Academy and Rajasthan Royals Colts & Sparks as a Sports Nutritionist.

    Through my practice, I strive to provide comprehensive and customized nutrition solutions that help my clients achieve their health goals. I firmly believe that with the right guidance and support, anyone can lead a healthy and fulfilling life.

    Terms and Conditions

    Payment Terms:
    The payment must be made in full at the time of registration. Accepted payment methods include cash, UPI, credit card, or cheque (subject to realization).

    Service Conditions:
    Services are non-refundable and non-transferable.
    Services cannot be exchanged with any other different packages.

    Renewals and Discretion:
    The grant of any service and renewals are solely at the discretion of ReviveQii Management.

    Service Duration:
    The period of service starts from the day of registration, not from the day of using the services.

    Program Start and Pause:
    The diet program or package cannot be held or paused once started.

    Unused Days:
    The unused number of days cannot be extended, and the amount will not be refunded if not used by the client for any reason.

    Medical Declarations:
    Clients with medical problems must declare ailments in writing. A doctor's certificate and medical reports need to be provided before starting any diet program or exercise program.

    Counseling Sessions:
    Diet and nutrition counseling sessions will be offered only on an appointment basis.
