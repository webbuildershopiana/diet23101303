    Profile

    I am Akshi Bhardwaj, an ACE Certified Nutritionist and fitness enthusiast. I also hold an ACE certification in Functional Training.

    With my online diet consultation services, I offer personalized diet plans and healthy meal plans for weight loss. I firmly believe that crash diets are not necessary to achieve a healthy and fit body. Instead, incorporating a variety of foods, especially those that one enjoys, is key to making the journey toward better health enjoyable.

    Through my personal fitness journey, I discovered the crucial role of nutrition in overall wellness. This realization inspired me to pursue in-depth studies in nutrition and fitness.

    As a Nutritionist and Dietitian, I am dedicated to sharing my knowledge and helping more individuals on their path to health and wellness. My goal is to make the process enjoyable and sustainable for all my clients.
