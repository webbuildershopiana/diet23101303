    Does Dt. Rajat Jain meet the clients personally?

    Yes, Dt. Rajat Jain will meet the client personally for the rst consultation.
    For further follow-ups, he will be expected to meet the client depending on the package taken by the client, or when there is a need to evaluate the reports.

    Do you suggest any blood tests?

    Yes, we suggest basic blood tests before enrollment and the number of tests may vary depending on the current medical or health issues.

    Do you give tablets or some products to lose weight?

    No, we dont recommend any products for losing weight.
    In fact, we give importance to local and traditional food readily available in your kitchen.
    We suggest a few vitamins along with a healthy & calculated diet plan depending on the health concern. We believe in a holistic approach while preparing a diet plan

    Can I start with a 3 months package and then extend it to 6 months?

    A program once enrolled in and can only be upgraded within the first week from the date of enrollment downgrades are not allowed.

    How much weight will I lose?

    Your body can safely lose 2-3 kilos in a month. Still, the actual results will depend upon your body system, metabolic rate, physical activity, and your commitment to following our diet plan to reach your goals.
    We focus more on FAT LOSS & INCH LOSS rather than just on numbers on the weighing scale

    Is it possible to reduce fat only from my thighs, arms, or belly?

    No. Spot reduction is not possible. One cannot target a specific body part and reduce fat only from there. Fat loss happens from all over the body proportionately.

    How will I get my diet plans?

    For online Services, your dietplan can be sent via WhatsApp/ Email. Whereas in Offline services, dietplan in a printed format can be given.

    Can I hold/pause my diet program/package in between?

    You cannot hold or pause the diet program/package once started.

    How is your diet program different from other diet programs?

    Its just not a diet program, its more of a learning process in which we work at educating our clients to understand their bodys requirements so that they can plan their own eating patterns and overcome the fears & myths associated with certain foods & diets.

    Can I join/continue a program if I am on a holiday?

    Yes, you can. Your location is not a barrier. All our programs offer regular WhatsApp consultations and we share all food plans via email.
    You will be educated on how to plan meals while traveling and assisted with choosing the best options when you are in a new country/city.

    I am a Non-Veg./Veg. Will it be considered?

    Whether you are non-vegetarian, vegetarian, vegan, eggitarian or you follow a Jain diet (like me) the diet will be made as per your likes and dislikes, and most importantly it will be doable.

    What is the pattern of your diet?

    We understand that people who follow very strict or limited diets cannot sustain them over a longer period.
    Just to give you a bit of an overview, our programs purely work on the basis of balanced healthy food and nutrition.
    We believe in creating a healthy lifestyle rather than promoting fads, crash diets & pills.

    an I pay fees in installments?

    Yes, you can make payment in two installments, but the second consultation will only begin after we have received the full payment.

    Can I get a refund if I am not willing to continue?

    Once you have paid for a program, the NO Refund policy applies. Due to the nature of the services involved, No refund is provided under any circumstances.

    What happens once I make the payments?

    For online clients, we generate an appointment with the selected nutritionist and send you a Questionnaire sheet.
    This contains all the info we will require from you to start the program.

    Will exercise guidance/consultation be included in the package?

    Yes, your current tness levels and exercise pattern will be analyzed rst and then we will guide you that what kind of workout you need to do as per your lifestyle and goals.

    What kind of exercise is recommended?

    The workout that suits your body and gives you an enjoyable experience.
    We suggest strength training but for you, it could be running, yoga, dancing, swimming, Zumba, cycling, etc.
