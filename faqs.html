    FAQs

    Does Dt. Rajat Jain meet the clients personally?

    Yes, Dt. Rajat Jain will meet the client personally for the ﬁrst consultation.
    For further follow-ups, he will be expected to meet the client depending on the package taken by the client, or when there is a need to evaluate the reports.

    Do you suggest any blood tests?

    Yes, we suggest basic blood tests before enrollment and the number of tests may vary depending on the current medical or health issues.

    Do you give tablets or some products to lose weight?

    No, we don’t recommend any products for losing weight.
    In fact, we give importance to local and traditional food readily available in your kitchen.
    We suggest a few vitamins along with a healthy & calculated diet plan depending on the health concern. We believe in a holistic approach while preparing a diet plan

    Can I start with a 3 months package and then extend it to 6 months?

    A program once enrolled in and can only be upgraded within the first week from the date of enrollment downgrades are not allowed.

    How much weight will I lose?

    Your body can safely lose 2-3 kilos in a month. Still, the actual results will depend upon your body system, metabolic rate, physical activity, and your commitment to following our diet plan to reach your goals.
    We focus more on FAT LOSS & INCH LOSS rather than just on numbers on the weighing scale

    Is it possible to reduce fat only from my thighs, arms, or belly?

    No. Spot reduction is not possible. One cannot target a specific body part and reduce fat only from there. Fat loss happens from all over the body proportionately.

    How will I get my diet plans?

    For online Services, your dietplan can be sent via WhatsApp/ Email. Whereas in Offline services, dietplan in a printed format can be given.

    Can I hold/pause my diet program/package in between?

    You cannot hold or pause the diet program/package once started.
