    Profile

    Hi, I’m Monisha Agarwal, as a certified Nutritionist and Dietitian from ACE, I specialize in Child Nutrition and PCOD and offer online diet consultations, personalized diet plans, and healthy meal plans for weight loss.

    I firmly believe that a poor diet is responsible for most health issues and that most diseases can be reversed or controlled by adopting the right diet and lifestyle. I am passionate about the transformative power of food and believe that it can be the most potent medicine or the worst poison depending on how it is consumed.

    My decision to study Nutrition was driven by my desire to help people around me. Through my online diet consultation services, I strive to empower my clients to achieve their health goals by providing personalized nutrition plans that cater to their unique needs and requirements.

    I firmly believe that getting fit does not require eating less; rather, it involves eating right. By working with me, my clients can be assured of a customized approach to diet and nutrition that emphasizes healthy, sustainable choices to promote optimal health and well-being.
