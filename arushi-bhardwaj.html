    Profile

    I am Arushi Bhardwaj, an ACE-certified fitness nutritionist, functional training specialist, and weight management expert. I offer online diet consultations, personalized diet plans, and healthy meal plans for weight loss.

    In addition to a Diploma in Dietetics Health & Nutrition, I hold certifications in sports nutrition and weight management from VLCC. My personal fitness journey led me to encounter various challenges, which sparked my interest in nutrition and fitness.

    As a firm believer in staying fit and healthy, I recognize the importance of physical and mental health, as they go hand in hand. I strongly believe that nutrition and exercise work in tandem, and healthy eating and proper training are key to achieving one’s fitness goals.

    My ultimate goal is to transform as many lives as possible and help individuals maintain a healthy and sustainable lifestyle for the long term. With my online diet consultation services, I strive to empower my clients to achieve their health goals through a personalized approach to nutrition and fitness.

    I offer online diet consultations and provide personalized diet plans and healthy meal plans for weight loss.

    My approach to nutrition is centered around eating well rather than eating less, and I believe that proper eating is the finest therapy for our health, but only when approached correctly.

    With 2 years of experience in the Nutrition, Wellness, and Fitness field, I have helped numerous individuals achieve their health goals through a customized approach to diet and nutrition.

    I firmly believe that a tailored nutrition plan, combined with guidance and support, can help individuals make positive changes in their lives. Through my online diet consultation services, I strive to empower my clients to take control of their health by providing personalized nutrition plans that cater to their unique needs and goals.
