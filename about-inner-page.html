    WHO WE ARE?
    Let's know about ReviveQii
    "Feeding the right emotion"
    Dietitian Rajat Jain started the Health Wealth Diet Clinic in 2016 with a mission to help people lead healthier and happier lives. With his extensive education and experience in the field of nutrition and dietetics, Rajat aimed to provide personalized and evidence-based dietary advice to individuals and families.
    In the early days of the clinic, Rajat worked tirelessly to establish a reputation for providing quality services and delivering results. He reached out to the local community, networking with health and wellness professionals, and educating people about the importance of a balanced diet.
    Rajat Jain
    Founder & Chief Dietititan, Clinical & Sports Nutrition Specialist, Certified Diabetes & Wellness Educator
    As word spread about the clinic’s success, more and more people started seeking its services. The clinic grew rapidly, and Rajat soon hired additional dietitians to keep up with demand. Today, the Health Wealth Diet Clinic is a thriving business with a team of skilled and passionate professionals who share Rajat’s commitment to helping people achieve their health and wellness goals. Now his clients include renowned names from across India and abroad like celebrities, designers, politicians, bureaucrats, sportspersons, etc.
    Rajat’s approach to nutrition emphasizes the importance of a balanced diet that includes a variety of healthy foods, portion control, and physical activity. He encourages his clients to make sustainable lifestyle changes rather than relying on fad diets or quick fixes.
    Through the years, the Health Wealth Diet Clinic has helped thousands of people improve their health, boost their energy levels, and achieve their desired body weight. Many of Rajat’s clients have reported significant reductions in their risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and various lifestyle disorders.
    Rajat is proud of the positive impact that the Health Wealth Diet Clinic has had on so many lives, and he continues to work tirelessly to help people achieve their health and wellness goals. He is dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest research and advancements in the field of nutrition and dietetics, and he remains committed to providing the highest quality services to his clients.
