    Nutritionist
    Hello, I'm Sandhya Joshi, a passionate and dedicated nutritionist with a deep commitment to helping individuals achieve their health and wellness goals through proper nutrition.
    See Plans

    Profile

    I had completed my course in Essentials of Weight Management and Supplements through CFA, and have achievement of transforming lives of many clients. With a background in weight/lifestyle management and child nutrition.

    I started my own fitness journey in 2018 and lost almost 30 kgs, this inspire me a lot to learn about nutrition and lifestyle management.

    I've assisted my clients in making their weight management journey easier by providing personalized and sustainable strategies tailored to their unique needs and goals. Here are some of the key ways I've helped them:

    Customized Meal Plans: I create individualized meal plans that take into account their dietary preferences, restrictions, and cultural factors. This makes it easier for them to stick to a healthier eating routine.

    Educating: I educate clients about the fundamentals of nutrition, helping them understand the impact of different foods on their weight.

    Behavior Modification: I work with clients to identify and address emotional and behavioral triggers for overeating, helping them develop healthier habits and coping mechanisms and setting realistic achievable goals.

    Ongoing Support: I provide continuous support and accountability through regular check-ins and adjustments to their plans as needed.

    Monitoring and Feedback: Regular monitoring of their progress allows for timely adjustments, and I provide constructive feedback to keep them motivated and also recognizing and celebrating each milestone achieved by my clients.

    By combining these strategies, I've helped my clients navigate their weight management journey with greater ease, leading to long-lasting results and improved overall well-being.
